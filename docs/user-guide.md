# Bountix User Guide

## Getting Started (2 minutes)
1. Install Freighter: [https://freighter.app](https://freighter.app)
2. Switch Freighter to the **Testnet** network.
3. Get free Testnet XLM: [https://friendbot.stellar.org/?addr=YOUR_ADDRESS](https://friendbot.stellar.org/?addr=YOUR_ADDRESS)
4. Visit [https://bountix.vercel.app](https://bountix.vercel.app)
5. Click **"Connect Wallet"** → approve the connection in Freighter.

## As a Poster (Post a Bounty)
1. Navigate to the Dashboard.
2. Click **"Post Bounty"**.
3. Fill in the details: Title, Description, Reward (XLM), and Deadline (days).
4. Sign the transaction in your wallet. The XLM is securely locked in the smart contract escrow.
5. Your bounty will appear on the board instantly.
6. When a hunter submits proof, review their work.
7. Click **Approve** (releases XLM to the hunter) or **Reject** (reopens the bounty for someone else).

## As a Hunter (Earn XLM)
1. Browse the open bounties on the Dashboard.
2. Click **"Claim"** on a bounty you can complete.
3. Complete the required task off-chain.
4. Click **"Submit Proof"** and paste the URL to your proof (e.g., a GitHub PR, a tweet, a Google Doc).
5. Wait for the poster to review. Once approved, the XLM is instantly transferred to your wallet!

## Dispute a Bounty
If the poster doesn't respond within a reasonable timeframe after you submit proof:
1. Open the bounty detail page.
2. Click **"Raise Dispute"** (requires Level 4 BNTX Reputation).
3. 3 community validators will be assigned to vote.
4. A 2-of-3 majority decides the outcome, releasing the funds fairly.

## FAQ
**Is this real money?**
No — this is currently running on the Stellar Testnet. XLM here is free and has no real-world value.

**Why do some actions cost no gas?**
Bountix leverages fee-bump transactions to sponsor gas fees for hunters, ensuring a frictionless onboarding experience.
